# Update 5.0

**Date:** November 5, 2024

![Face Swap](/blog/update-5.0-1.png)

## 1. Face Swap Function

Now you can replace a face on any image. Functionality works with images generated on site, personal gallery items or user-uploaded photos. Function consumes one generation when activated.

## 2. Personal Gallery Pagination

Users can now click the icon next to column display slider to access page numbers, allowing navigation through entire image gallery collection.

## 3. New Max Plan

Premium subscription offering 600 daily generations for power users who need maximum access. This plan gets experimental features first.

## 4. Subscription Restoration

Platform switched to T-Bank payment services. Monthly subscriptions with auto-renewal returned, offering approximately 20% savings compared to one-time payments. New card additions include a test payment of 10 rubles that is immediately refunded.

## 5. Visual Design Update

Logo, site icon and element colors updated.

## 6. Page Title and URL System

Improved title generation and URL structure.

## 7. Minor Fixes

Numerous small improvements and fixes across the platform.
