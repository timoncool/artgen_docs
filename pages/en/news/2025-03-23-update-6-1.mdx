# Update 6.1

**Date:** March 23, 2025

## 1. Style Names, Categories and Descriptions

![Style categories](/blog/update-6.1-2.png)

Styles are now organized into 10 categories:

- **ART** — Artistic styles (traditional and digital art)
- **PHOTO** — Photography (photographic styles and techniques)
- **DIGIT** — Digital art (computer graphics)
- **GAME** — Game graphics (video game styles)
- **PAPER** — Paper art (paper art techniques)
- **FUTURE** — Future (futuristic/sci-fi styles)
- **ADS** — Advertising (commercial visual styles)
- **TRAD** — Traditions (classical art techniques)
- **FX** — Effects (visual effects and enhancements)
- **CULT** — Culture (ethnic and cultural styles)
