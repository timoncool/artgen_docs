# Update 7.5

**Date:** October 13, 2025

![New models](/blog/update-7.5-1.png)

## 1. New AI Models

Many of the latest models added. From Juggernaut Base Flux and HiDream-i1 to newest image editing models Seedream 4.0 and FLUX.1 Kontext [dev].

Pay attention to generation costs and model operation modes.

![Background image rating](/blog/update-7.5-2.png)

## 2. Background Image Rating

Background image rating feature added. Leave a rating if you like a background image, we'll show the best ones more often and disable unsuccessful ones.

![Favorite resources](/blog/update-7.5-3.png)

## 3. Favorite Resources

Favorite models, styles and LoRAs marking feature added. Just click the star, and all starred models will appear in a separate sort.

![Account linking](/blog/update-7.5-4.png)

## 4. Account Linking

Account linking with quick authorization methods added. Now you can add additional login methods to your profile and disable unnecessary ones.

## 5. Fixes

Fixed old bugs and added new ones. Many small fixes and performance improvements.
