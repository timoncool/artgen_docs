# Performance Mode

![Performance Mode](/images/blog/img_020.png)

Choose between speed and quality.

## Available Modes

### Fast
- Quickest generation
- Lower quality
- Good for testing ideas

### Balanced
- Moderate speed
- Good quality
- Recommended for most uses

### Quality
- Slower generation
- Best quality
- For final results

### Ultra
- Slowest generation
- Maximum quality
- For professional work

## Mode Selection

Choose based on your needs:
- Experimenting → Fast
- Regular use → Balanced
- Final images → Quality/Ultra

## Queue Priority

Higher subscription tiers get priority in the generation queue.
