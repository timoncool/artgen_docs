# Registration on ArtGeneration.me

To use all the features of the platform, you need to register.

## Registration Methods

### Via Social Networks
The fastest way is authorization via social networks:
- **VKontakte** — for users from Russia
- **Yandex** — for users from Russia

### Via Email
You can also register using your email address.

## Registration Process

import { Steps } from '@/components/mdx'

<Steps>
### Step 1
Go to the [main page](https://artgeneration.me) and click the "Login" button

### Step 2
Choose the authorization method: social network or email

### Step 3
Confirm access to your account (for social networks) or enter the confirmation code (for email)

### Step 4
Done! Now you can use all the features of the platform
</Steps>

## After Registration

After successful registration, you will have access to:
- Image generation
- Saving works in your personal gallery
- Creating folders to organize images
- Access to generation history
- Ability to purchase a subscription

## Free Plan

New users receive free generations to familiarize themselves with the platform. Read more about plans in the [Payments & Subscriptions](/docs/payments) section.
