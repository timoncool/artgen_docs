# Account & Payment FAQ

Questions about accounts, subscriptions, and payments.

## How do I recover my password?

1. Click "Forgot password?" on login page
2. Enter your email
3. Check your inbox for reset link
4. Create new password

## How do I change my email?

Contact support at support@artgeneration.me to change your account email.

## What payment methods are accepted?

Currently accepted:
- Bank cards (Russian cards only)
- Electronic wallets

## Will I lose my generations if I cancel?

No. Your purchased generations never expire. Daily generations reset each day regardless of subscription status.

## How do I get a refund?

Contact support@artgeneration.me with your payment details. Refunds are processed within 5-10 business days.

## What's included in PRO subscription?

- 300 daily generations (vs 50 free)
- Priority queue
- Access to all models
- Advanced features (face swap, etc.)

## Do unused generations carry over?

Daily generations reset each day and don't accumulate. Purchased generation packages never expire.
