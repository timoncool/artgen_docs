## Quick Answers

### How do I start generating images?
Register on the platform, get your daily generations, and go to the "Create" section.

### How many generations do I get?
Free users get 50 daily generations. PRO subscribers get 300 daily generations.

### Can I use images commercially?
Yes, all images you create belong to you and can be used commercially.
