# User Menu

The user menu provides access to account settings and personal features.

## Menu Sections

### Profile
View and edit profile information:
- Username
- Avatar
- Email

### My Works
Quick access to your personal gallery of generated images.

### Folders
Manage folders for organizing images.

### Subscription
Information about your current subscription and the ability to change it.

### Balance
View generation balance and transaction history.

### Settings
Personal platform settings:
- Interface language
- Theme
- Notifications

### Current Plan
Your current plan and the date of the next charge (for auto-renewal subscriptions) are displayed.

### Changing Plans
You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, the difference will be charged immediately.

## Logging Out

To log out of your account, click the "Log Out" button at the bottom of the menu.
