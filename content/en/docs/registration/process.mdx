# Registration Process

![Registration Process](/screens/quick-login-settings.png)

Registration on ArtGeneration.me is a quick and simple process that takes less than a minute. The platform offers several ways to create an account at your choice.

## Registration Methods

### Quick Login via VK

The fastest way for VKontakte users:

1. Click the "Sign in with VK" button
2. Confirm access in the VKontakte window
3. Done — your account is created automatically

**Advantages:**
- No need to create a new password
- Automatic profile filling (name, avatar)
- Quick one-click login in the future

### Quick Login via Yandex

Similar process for Yandex users:

1. Click the "Sign in with Yandex" button
2. Confirm access in the Yandex ID window
3. Account is created and ready to use

### Registration via Email

Classic registration method:

<Steps>
### Click "Sign Up"

### Enter your email address

### Create a strong password (minimum 8 characters)

### Confirm email by clicking the link in the email

### Fill out your profile (optional)
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning">
  **Password recommendations:**
  - Use a combination of letters, numbers, and symbols
  - Don't use simple passwords like "123456" or "password"
  - Don't use the same password for different services
</Callout>

## Account Confirmation

When registering via email, you'll receive an email with a confirmation link. If the email didn't arrive:

- Check your "Spam" folder
- Make sure the email was entered correctly
- Request a resend
- Wait a few minutes — delivery sometimes takes time

## Bot Protection

If the system suspects automated registration, you may be asked to complete a captcha. This is a standard security measure to protect the platform.

## Password Recovery

If you forgot your password:

<Steps>
### On the login page, click "Forgot password?"

### Enter the email used during registration

### Receive an email with a password reset link

### Follow the link and create a new password
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
  The reset link is valid for 24 hours.
</Callout>

## After Registration

After successful registration, you will automatically:
- Receive a welcome bonus (3 days of PRO + 100 generations)
- Be redirected to the generator page
- Be able to start creating images right away

## Possible Problems

<Callout type="error">
  **Email already registered** — you may have already created an account earlier. Try logging in or recovering your password.
</Callout>

<Callout type="warning">
  **Error during social network authorization:**
  - Check if pop-ups are blocked in your browser
  - Try a different browser
  - Contact support if the problem persists
</Callout>

<Callout type="warning">
  **Confirmation email not arriving:**
  - Verify the email is correct
  - Check your "Spam" folder
  - Request a resend after 5 minutes
</Callout>
