# Registration Process

Registration on ArtGeneration takes just a few minutes.

## Registration Methods

### Via Email
1. Click "Sign Up"
2. Enter your email
3. Create a password
4. Confirm email via link

### Via Social Networks
Quick registration through:
- Google
- VK
- Yandex

## After Registration

After successful registration you will:
- Receive welcome bonus generations
- Get access to all basic features
- Be able to save your creations
