# Why Register

Registration opens access to all platform features.

## Benefits of Registration

### Save Your Work
All your generations are saved in your personal gallery.

### History and Favorites
Access to generation history and ability to add to favorites.

### Personalization
Save your preferred settings and styles.

### Bonuses
Receive daily free generations and participate in promotions.

### Community
Ability to publish works and interact with other users.

## Without Registration

Without registration you can:
- View the public gallery
- Learn about platform features

But you won't be able to create your own images.
