# Why Register?

![Why Register](/screens/profile-menu.png)

Registration on ArtGeneration.me opens access to the full functionality of the platform and many benefits unavailable to guest users.

## Saving Your Work

**Generation History:**
- All your generations are automatically saved in the cloud
- Access your history from any device
- Ability to return to any work and create a new version
- View generation parameters to replicate results

**Collection Organization:**
- Add your best works to favorites
- Create folders for thematic organization
- Sort and filter by various parameters

## Personalization

**Default Settings:**
- Save your preferred models and styles
- Configure generation parameters for your needs
- Create presets for quick access

**Interface:**
- Choose interface language
- Customize gallery display
- Personal notifications

## Social Features

**Publishing Works:**
- Share generations in the public gallery
- Receive likes and comments from the community
- Inspire other users

**Interaction:**
- Follow interesting creators
- Track their new works in your feed
- Create versions of images you like

**Creator Profile:**
- Personal page with your works
- View and like statistics
- Ability to tell about yourself
