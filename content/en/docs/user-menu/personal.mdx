# Personal

The "Personal" section is your personal dashboard for managing all created images, likes, favorites, and subscriptions.

## Account Information

The top of the section displays basic information about your profile:

- **Username** — your nickname on the platform
- **Subscription time** — validity period of your current subscription
- **Likes** — number of likes received on your works
- **Favorites** — how many times your works have been added to favorites
- **Followers** — number of users subscribed to you

<Callout type="info">
Statistics are updated in real-time and help track the popularity of your works.
</Callout>

## View Tabs

The "Personal" section contains four main tabs for content navigation:

### Created

All images you have generated. This is where your complete work history is stored, organized by creation date.

### Likes

Images from other users that you have liked. A convenient way to return to works you enjoyed.

### Favorites

Works you have added to favorites for quick access. Use this tab to save particularly inspiring images.

### Subscriptions

Content from users you follow. Keep track of your favorite creators' work in one place.

## Sorting Categories

Four sorting modes are available for convenient image search:

- **Fresh** — images sorted by creation date, from newest to oldest
- **Interesting** — works that have received activity recently
- **Popular** — images with the highest number of likes and favorites
- **Best** — top works based on all metrics combined

<Callout type="info">
Switch between sorting modes to find the images you need faster.
</Callout>

## Date Folders

All your images are automatically grouped by creation day:

- **Automatic grouping** — images are organized into folders by date without any additional actions
- **Convenient navigation** — quick access to works from a specific day
- **Delete by day** — ability to delete all images for a specific day with one action

<Steps>
### Find the desired date
Folders are displayed in chronological order.

### Expand the folder
Click on the date to view images from that day.

### Delete if necessary
Use the folder delete button to clear all images for the selected day.
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning">
Deleting a day folder removes all images in it. The action can be undone within one hour.
</Callout>

## Bulk Operations

Use bulk operations to work with multiple images at once:

### Selecting Images

Mark the desired images with checkboxes to perform group actions. The checkbox appears when hovering over a thumbnail.

### Floating Action Bar

When one or more images are selected, a panel with available actions appears at the bottom of the screen:

- **Number selected** — displays the count of marked images
- **Delete selected** — delete all marked images
- **Deselect all** — cancel selection of all images

<Steps>
### Mark images
Click on the checkboxes of the desired images.

### Wait for the panel to appear
The floating bar will appear automatically.

### Choose an action
Click "Delete selected" to delete.

### Confirm the action
Confirm deletion if required.
</Steps>

## Navigation and Search

### Pagination

Images are divided into pages for convenient navigation:

- Navigate between pages using arrows
- Display of current page number
- Quick jump to first and last page

### Prompt Filter

Use the search bar to filter images by prompt text:

- Enter keywords from the prompt
- Results update instantly
- Search works across all your images

<Callout type="info">
Searching by prompts helps quickly find images created with specific parameters or keywords.
</Callout>

## Image Actions

The following actions are available for each image:

### Deletion

Delete unwanted images from your collection. After deletion, the image can be restored within one hour.

<Callout type="warning">
After one hour, restoring a deleted image is impossible. Make sure you are not deleting important works.
</Callout>

### Privacy Settings

Manage the visibility of your images:

- **Public** (open eye) — image is visible to all users in the gallery
- **Private** (closed eye) — image is visible only to you

<Steps>
### Find the image
Hover over the desired image.

### Click the eye icon
The icon is located on the action panel.

### Toggle visibility
The status will change instantly.
</Steps>

### Viewing

Open an image for detailed viewing:

- Full-size mode
- Generation parameter information
- Prompt text
- Creation date
- Likes and favorites statistics

<Callout type="info">
In viewing mode, all standard actions are available: downloading, creating similar, quality enhancement, and more.
</Callout>
