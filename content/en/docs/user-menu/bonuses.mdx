# Bonuses and Referral Program

The "Bonuses" section allows you to activate the referral program and invite friends to ArtGeneration.me. You receive rewards for each invited user, and your friends get nice bonuses upon registration.

## How the Referral Program Works

<Steps>
### Open the "Bonuses" section
Go to the user menu and select the "Bonuses" section.

### Copy your referral link
Copy your unique referral link to invite friends.

### Share the link
Send the link to friends or post it on social media.

### Receive bonuses
When friends register using your link, you both receive bonuses.
</Steps>

## What the Invited User Gets

Every user who registers using your referral link receives:

- **7 days of subscription** instead of the standard 3 days
- **200 generations as a gift**

<Callout type="info">
Bonuses are automatically credited upon registration via the referral link.
</Callout>

## What You Get

For each invited user, you receive:

- **20% of all purchased generations** by your referral
- **20% of all purchased subscription days** by your referral

<Callout type="tip">
Bonuses are credited for the entire time the invited user makes purchases on the site.
</Callout>

## Referral Statistics

On the right side of the "Bonuses" section, you can see statistics of your invited users:

- Number of registered referrals
- Total amount of received bonuses
- Crediting history

<Callout type="warning">
Statistics are updated once a day. New data may appear with a delay of up to 24 hours.
</Callout>
