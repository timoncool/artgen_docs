## Reporting Issues

When reporting a problem:
1. Describe the issue clearly
2. Include screenshots if possible
3. Mention your browser and device
4. Provide generation ID if relevant

## Response Times

- Live chat: minutes
- Email: 24-48 hours
- Complex issues: up to 5 business days
