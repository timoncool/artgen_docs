# Payments

import { Callout, Steps } from '@/components/mdx'

The "Payments" section in your account allows you to manage subscriptions, payment methods, and view the history of all transactions.

## Cancel Auto-Renewal

If you want to cancel automatic subscription renewal:

<Steps>
### Open the Payments section
Go to the user menu and select "Payments".

### Find your active subscription
The section displays your current subscription with information about the next billing date.

### Cancel auto-renewal
Click the "Cancel Auto-Renewal" button or similar option.

### Confirm the action
Confirm the cancellation in the popup window.
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
After canceling auto-renewal, your subscription will remain active until the end of the paid period. You can continue using all benefits until the expiration date.
</Callout>

## Remove Payment Method

You can remove a saved card or other payment method from your account.

<Steps>
### Go to Payments
Open the "Payments" section in your account.

### Find payment methods
Navigate to the list of saved payment methods.

### Remove the method
Click the delete button next to the payment method you want to remove.
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning">
**Important:** You cannot remove a payment method while auto-renewal is active. First cancel auto-renewal, then remove the card.
</Callout>

## Payment History

The "Payments" section includes a table with all your transactions:

| Information | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| Date | Date and time of the payment |
| Amount | Payment amount in your currency |
| Type | Subscription, balance top-up, or purchase |
| Status | Successful, processing, or declined |
| Description | Transaction details |

<Callout type="info">
Payment history is saved for the entire lifetime of your account. You can use it for expense tracking or when contacting support.
</Callout>
