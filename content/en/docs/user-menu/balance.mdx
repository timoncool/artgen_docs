# Balance & Generations

View and manage your generation balance.

## Balance Display

Your current balance shows:
- Free generations (daily)
- Purchased generations
- Subscription generations

## Getting Generations

### Daily Free
Receive free generations every day.

### Purchase Packages
Buy generation packages for one-time use.

### Subscribe
Get monthly generations with subscription.
