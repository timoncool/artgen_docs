# Payments & Subscriptions

ArtGeneration.me offers various plans for different user needs.

## Plans

### FREE
- Limited number of generations per day
- Basic models
- Standard resolution
- Watermark on images

### LIGHT
- Increased generation limit
- Access to more models
- No watermark
- Priority queue

### PRO
- Large generation limit
- All models and styles
- Face Swap
- Upscale
- Priority support

### MAX
- Maximum generation limit
- All PRO features
- Exclusive models
- Maximum priority

## Payment FAQ

### How do I cancel my subscription?
Go to user menu → Subscription → Cancel subscription.

### What happens when I cancel?
You retain access until the end of the paid period.

### Can I change my plan?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time.
