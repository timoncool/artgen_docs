# Terms of Use

By using the ArtGeneration.me platform, you agree to follow these rules.

## Prohibited Content

### It is strictly prohibited to generate:
- Child pornography or sexualization of minors
- Realistic images of violence and cruelty
- Content inciting hatred based on race, nationality, religion
- Images of real people in compromising situations without their consent
- Content that infringes copyright
- Instructions for creating weapons or drugs

### Restricted content:
- NSFW content is only allowed in private mode
- Images of public figures must be clearly artistic

## Service Usage

### Allowed:
- Generating images for personal use
- Commercial use (with a paid subscription)
- Publishing works with indication that they were created using AI

### Prohibited:
- Automated access to the service without permission
- Attempts to bypass restrictions and security mechanisms
- Reselling access to the service
- Use for spam or fraud

## Copyright

### Your rights:
- You retain rights to prompts you create
- With a paid subscription, you get rights to commercial use of images

### Platform rights:
- The platform may use public images to improve the service
- Private images are not used without your consent

## Moderation

All public images are moderated. Images that violate the rules will be removed, and the account may be blocked.

## Changes to Rules

The platform reserves the right to change the rules. Users will be notified of significant changes.
