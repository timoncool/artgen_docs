# Prompt Tips

Practical recommendations for writing effective prompts.

## Be Specific

Instead of "a cat", write "a fluffy orange tabby cat sitting on a windowsill".

## Use Descriptive Adjectives

Add adjectives for:
- Colors (vibrant, muted, golden)
- Textures (smooth, rough, glossy)
- Mood (peaceful, dramatic, mysterious)

## Specify Style

Mention artistic style:
- Photorealistic
- Digital art
- Oil painting
- Anime style
- Watercolor

## Add Lighting

Describe lighting:
- Golden hour
- Studio lighting
- Dramatic shadows
- Soft diffused light

## Avoid Negations

Instead of "no people", use negative prompt for exclusions.

## Experiment

Try different phrasings and combinations to find what works best.
