# Generation History

Generation history is the section where all your created images are displayed.

## Image Display

All your images are displayed in chronological order — from newest to oldest.

## Search

Use the search function to find images by prompt text. Simply enter keywords, and the system will show matching generations.

## Mobile Version

<Callout type="info">
  On mobile devices and small screens, the history section is hidden by default. Click on the panel to expand it.
</Callout>

## Image Actions

When working with images, the following actions are available:

**Deletion** — delete unwanted images from history.

**Preview** — hover over the magnifying glass icon to see an enlarged image.

**Fullscreen Mode** — open the image in fullscreen for detailed viewing.

**Privacy Settings** — click the eye icon to manage image visibility for other users.

## Image Statistics

Each image displays counters for likes and favorites from other users.
