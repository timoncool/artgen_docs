# Favorites

![Favorites](/screens/history-panel-full.png)

The "Favorites" section is your personal collection of best works. Here you store images you want quick access to: successful generations, references for future projects, inspiring works.

**Quick Access** â€” no need to search for successful generations in a long history. All your best works are collected in one place.

**References** â€” save images whose style or composition you want to use in the future.

**Portfolio** â€” build a collection of your best works for demonstration.

## Adding to Favorites

**From Generation History:**

<Steps>
### Hover Over the Image

Hover your cursor over an image in the history.

### Click the Heart Icon

Click on the heart icon.

### Done

The image is instantly added to favorites.
</Steps>

**From Image View:**

<Steps>
### Open the Image

Open the image in full-size mode.

### Add to Favorites

Click the "Add to Favorites" button on the action panel.
</Steps>

**From Gallery:**

<Steps>
### Find an Image

Find an image you like in the public gallery.

### Add to Favorites

Click the heart to add to favorites.
</Steps>

## Viewing Favorites

Go to the "Favorites" tab on the left panel of the generator. Images are displayed as a grid of thumbnails, sorted by date added.

## Working with Favorites

**Search** â€” use the search field to find images by keywords from the prompt.

**Sorting** â€” change the display order: by date added, by creation date, by model.

**Filtering** â€” filter favorites by model, style, or other parameters.

## Image Actions

When working with images in favorites, all standard actions are available:

- View in full size
- Copy generation parameters
- Move to folder
- Download image
- Remove from favorites

## Removing from Favorites

To remove an image from favorites:
- Click the heart again (it will become empty)
- Or use the context menu and select "Remove from Favorites"

Removing from favorites does not delete the image itself â€” it remains in generation history.
