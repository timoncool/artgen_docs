# Folders

Folders allow you to organize your images for convenient access.

## Image Organization

Use folders to group images by projects, themes, or any other criteria.

## Working with Images in Folders

When working with images in folders, standard actions are available:

- **Deletion** — delete the image
- **Preview** — hover over the magnifying glass icon for a quick preview
- **Fullscreen Mode** — open the image in fullscreen
- **Privacy Settings** — eye icon for managing visibility

## Search

Use prompt text search to find the images you need within folders.

<Callout type="info">
  On mobile devices, the section is hidden by default. Click to expand.
</Callout>
