# Weight Tips

Tips for effective use of weights.

## Start Small

Begin with small adjustments (1.1-1.2) and increase gradually.

## Don't Overuse

Too many weights can make the prompt confusing. Focus on key elements.

## Test Incrementally

Change one weight at a time to understand its effect.

## Combine with Negative Prompt

Use weights for emphasis, negative prompt for exclusion.

## Document Your Findings

Keep notes on what weight values work best for different scenarios.

## Consider the Model

Some models are more sensitive to weights than others. Adjust accordingly.
