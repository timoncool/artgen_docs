# Weights

Weights allow you to control the importance of different elements in your prompt.

## What Are Weights?

Weights are numerical values that indicate how much attention the model should pay to a particular part of the prompt.

## Why Use Weights?

- Emphasize important elements
- Reduce influence of secondary details
- Fine-tune the result
