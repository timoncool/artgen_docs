# Face Swap

![Face Swap](/screens/face-swap-panel.png)

ArtGeneration.me offers a face replacement feature for images. This tool allows you to replace a face in any generation or uploaded image with another face of your choice. Face swap can be activated in several situations.

## When creating a new generation

![Face swap area and resolution selection interface](/guide/face-swap-01.png)

In the right sidebar, find the "Face Swap" section and upload a photo with the face you want to use. After the image is generated, the system will automatically apply the replacement, and both the result with face swap and without will be displayed as usual.

## To an existing generation

![Face swap interface](/images/blog/img_018.png)

<Steps>
### Select the image
Select the desired image in the gallery in the left sidebar

### Upload a face photo
Upload a photo with the desired face

### Start the replacement
Click the "Swap Now" button

![Face swap process](/guide/face-swap-02.png)

### Wait for the result
Wait for processing to complete

![Face swap result](/guide/face-swap-03.png)
</Steps>

## To your own uploaded image

<Steps>
### Reset results
Click the "Reset" button to clear the generation results

### Upload your image
Upload an image through the "Own Image" feature

### Confirm the action
If there is no active generation, the system will warn that the replacement will be applied to the uploaded image

### Start the replacement
Upload a photo with the new face and click "Swap Now"
</Steps>

## Photo requirements

For best results, use a photo that meets the following criteria:

- **Human face only** â€” the technology does not work with animals or fantasy creatures
- **Preferably head only** on a solid background
- **Neutral facial expression**
- **Open eyes**
- **Good even lighting** without strong shadows
- **Frontal angle** (like a passport photo, but in color)
- **Clear, non-blurry image**

<Callout type="warning">
Face swap technology only works with human faces. Attempts to replace faces of anthropomorphic animals, anime characters, monsters, or other creatures may display an error or produce poor-quality results.
</Callout>

## Technical parameters

<Callout type="info">
- **Supported formats:** jpeg, png, webp
- **Maximum file size:** 5 MB
- **Feature available by subscription**
</Callout>

- The feature works with both generated images and those uploaded through "Own Image" or results of Upscale or outpainting
- If there are multiple faces in the photo, the leftmost one will be replaced

## Usage recommendations

- Use photos with similar angles for better results
- If needed, you can repeat the operation again if you're not satisfied with the result
- The quality of the replacement directly depends on the quality of both source photos
