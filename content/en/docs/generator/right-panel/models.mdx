# Models

Models are the foundation for image generation. Each model has unique characteristics.

## Model Types

### Realistic
Models for photorealistic images, portraits, landscapes.

### Anime
Models for anime and manga style images.

### Artistic
Models for artistic styles, illustrations, digital art.

### Universal
Models suitable for various tasks.

## Choosing a Model

Consider:
- Desired style
- Subject matter
- Quality requirements
- Generation speed

## Model Updates

New models are added regularly. Check the "New" section for latest additions.
