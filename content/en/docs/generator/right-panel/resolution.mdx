# Resolution

Choose the size and aspect ratio of generated images.

## Standard Resolutions

- **512x512** — square, fast
- **768x768** — square, detailed
- **1024x1024** — high quality square

## Aspect Ratios

- **1:1** — square (social media posts)
- **16:9** — widescreen (desktop wallpapers)
- **9:16** — vertical (phone wallpapers)
- **4:3** — classic (photos)
- **3:2** — photography standard

## Custom Resolution

Enter custom width and height within limits.

## Upscaling

Generate at lower resolution, then upscale for better results and faster generation.

## Generation Cost

Higher resolutions may cost more generations.
