# Styles

Styles modify the visual appearance of generated images.

## Style Categories

### Artistic
- Oil painting
- Watercolor
- Pencil sketch
- Digital art

### Photographic
- Portrait
- Landscape
- Macro
- Street photography

### Stylized
- Anime
- Cartoon
- Pixel art
- 3D render

## Applying Styles

Select a style from the list or search by name. Multiple styles can be combined.

## Style Strength

Adjust style influence using the strength slider.

## Custom Styles

Save your favorite style combinations for quick access.
