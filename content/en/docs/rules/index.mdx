# Platform Rules

Rules and guidelines for using ArtGeneration.me.

## Overview

By using ArtGeneration.me, you agree to follow these rules. Violations may result in account suspension or termination.

## Key Principles

1. **Respect others** — Don't create content that harms or harasses others
2. **Follow the law** — Don't create illegal content
3. **Be honest** — Don't misrepresent yourself or your work
4. **Protect privacy** — Don't share others' personal information

## Reporting Violations

If you see content that violates our rules, use the "Report" button on the image or contact support@artgeneration.me.

## Consequences

Violations may result in:
- Content removal
- Temporary suspension
- Permanent ban
- Legal action (for serious violations)
