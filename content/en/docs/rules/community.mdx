# Community Guidelines

Guidelines for interacting with the ArtGeneration.me community.

## Be Respectful

- Treat others with kindness
- Give constructive feedback
- Celebrate others' creativity
- Help newcomers learn

## No Harassment

Prohibited behaviors:
- Bullying or intimidation
- Stalking or unwanted contact
- Threats of any kind
- Discriminatory language

## No Spam

Don't:
- Post repetitive content
- Send unsolicited messages
- Promote external services excessively
- Use bots for engagement

## Honest Representation

- Don't claim others' work as your own
- Credit inspirations when appropriate
- Be transparent about AI-generated content
- Don't mislead about your identity

## Reporting Issues

If you experience harassment or see rule violations:
1. Use the "Report" button
2. Block the user if needed
3. Contact support for serious issues

## Consequences

Community guideline violations may result in:
- Warning
- Temporary mute
- Temporary ban
- Permanent ban

Severity depends on the nature and frequency of violations.
