# Content Rules

Guidelines for what content is allowed on the platform.

## Prohibited Content

The following content is strictly prohibited:

### Illegal Content
- Child exploitation material (zero tolerance)
- Content promoting terrorism
- Content promoting violence
- Illegal drug manufacturing instructions

### Harmful Content
- Non-consensual intimate images
- Harassment or bullying
- Hate speech targeting protected groups
- Doxxing or sharing private information

### Deceptive Content
- Impersonation of real people without consent
- Deepfakes intended to deceive
- Misinformation that could cause harm

## NSFW Content

Adult content (NSFW) is allowed with restrictions:
- Must be marked as NSFW
- Must not appear in public galleries by default
- Must not involve real people without consent
- Must comply with all other rules

## Moderation

All content is subject to:
- Automatic AI moderation
- Manual review when flagged
- Community reporting

## Appeals

If your content was incorrectly removed, contact support@artgeneration.me with:
- Your username
- Description of the content
- Reason you believe removal was incorrect
