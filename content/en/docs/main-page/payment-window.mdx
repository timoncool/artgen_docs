# Payment Window

The payment window allows you to purchase PRO access or additional generations.

## Accessing the Payment Window

The payment window can be opened in several ways:

<Steps>
### Through the Header Button

Click on the "Claim 600" button at the top of the site.

### Through the Coin Counter

Click on the coin counter in the upper right corner.

### Through the User Menu

Go to the "Payments" section in the user dropdown menu.
</Steps>

## Payment Window Tabs

The payment window contains three tabs:

### 1. One-time Payment

PRO access without automatic renewal. You pay once and get PRO access for the selected period without needing to track your subscription.

### 2. Subscriptions

Subscription with automatic renewal and a constant **20%** discount from the base price.

Additional discounts for longer periods:

| Period | Additional Discount |
|--------|---------------------|
| 3 months | 10% |
| 6 months | 15% |
| 1 year | 20% |

<Callout type="info">
  Subscription is the most cost-effective way to use ArtGen if you plan to generate images regularly.
</Callout>

### 3. Generation Packages

Additional generation packages that **never expire**. The following options are available:

- **500 generations** — starter pack
- **2,500 generations** — medium pack
- **8,000 generations** — large pack

<Callout type="info">
  Purchased generations are only consumed after your daily limit runs out.
</Callout>

## Daily Generation Limits
