# Payment Window

The payment window allows you to purchase a subscription or additional coins.

## Accessing the Payment Window

The payment window can be opened in several ways:

<Steps>
### Through the Header Button

Click on the "Claim 600" button at the top of the site.

### Through the Coin Counter

Click on the coin counter in the upper right corner.

### Through the User Menu

Go to the "Payments" section in the user dropdown menu.
</Steps>

## Plans and Subscriptions

![Plans and Subscriptions](/screens/subscription-plans.png)

Two subscription tiers are available:

### Pro
- Daily coins
- Access to an extended set of models
- Multiple images at once
- Additional styles and LoRA

### Max
- Maximum number of daily coins
- Access to all models, including NSFW
- Maximum number of images at once
- All styles and LoRA

## Purchasing Coins

![Purchasing Coins](/screens/purchase-coins.png)

For one-time purchases, coin packs are available:

- **500 coins** — starter pack
- **2,500 coins** — medium pack
- **8,000 coins** — large pack

![Pack Selection](/screens/purchase-coins2.png)

The price of each pack is displayed next to the "Pay" button.

<Callout type="info">
  Purchased coins never expire and are only consumed after your free daily coins run out.
</Callout>

## Payment Methods

- Bank cards (Visa, MasterCard, MIR)
- Electronic wallets
- Other payment methods

## Daily Bonus

![Daily Bonus](/screens/daily-reward-popup.png)

Every day you can get **600 coins for free**! Simply visit the site and click "Claim" in the pop-up window.

![Claiming Bonus](/screens/daily-reward-popup2.png)

The bonus resets every 24 hours.

<Callout type="info">
  Don't forget to visit the site every day to claim your free bonus!
</Callout>
