# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### What is ArtGeneration.me?
ArtGeneration.me is a platform for creating images using artificial intelligence. You describe the desired image in text, and the neural network generates it.

### Do I need to pay to use it?
There is a free plan with a limited number of generations. Paid subscriptions are available for full access to all features.

### What language should I write prompts in?
We recommend writing in English for best results, as most models are trained on English data.

## Image Generation

### Why doesn't the result match my prompt?
Try:
- Making the prompt more detailed
- Using a negative prompt
- Trying a different model
- Changing the seed

### How long does generation take?
Usually 10-30 seconds depending on the selected quality mode and server load.

### Can I use generated images commercially?
Yes, with a paid subscription you get rights to commercial use of generated images.

## Technical Questions

### What is the maximum resolution?
The maximum resolution depends on the plan and selected model. With the Upscale feature, you can increase the image up to 4K.

### Why won't generation start?
Check:
- Are you logged in
- Do you have generations in your balance
- Have you exceeded the daily limit

### How do I recover a deleted image?
Unfortunately, deleted images cannot be recovered. We recommend downloading important works to your device.

## Payment

### What payment methods are available?
Bank cards, YooMoney, SberPay for Russia. Cards and PayPal for international users.

### How do I cancel my subscription?
User menu → Subscription → Cancel subscription.

### Is a refund possible?
Yes, within 14 days of payment if services have not been used.
