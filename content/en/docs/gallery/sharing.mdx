# Sharing

Share your works with other users.

## Getting a Link

1. Open the image
2. Click "Share"
3. Copy the link

## Privacy Settings

- **Public** — visible to everyone
- **By Link** — only those with link
- **Private** — only you

## Social Networks

Share directly to:
- Telegram
- VK
- Twitter

## Embedding

Get embed code for placing on other sites.
