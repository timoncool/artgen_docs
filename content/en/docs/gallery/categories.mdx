# Gallery Categories

![Gallery Categories](/screens/gallery-filters-bar.png)

The ArtGeneration.me gallery is organized by categories for easy navigation and finding inspiration. Categories help quickly find images of a specific theme.

## Main Categories

**All** — the complete collection of public images without filtering. Here you'll find all the variety of user works.

**New** — recently uploaded works. A great way to follow fresh content and new trends.

**Popular** — images with the most likes. The best works selected by the community.

**Top** — best works for a specific period: day, week, month, or all time.

## Thematic Categories

**Portraits** — images of people, characters, close-up faces. Includes both realistic and stylized portraits.

**Landscapes** — nature, environment, views. From photorealistic to fantastic landscapes.

**Fantasy** — fantastic worlds, magical creatures, epic scenes. Dragons, elves, magical castles.

**Sci-Fi** — science fiction, space, futuristic technologies. Spaceships, cyberpunk, robots.

**Anime** — images in Japanese animation style. Characters, scenes, illustrations in anime style.

**Abstract** — abstract art, patterns, textures. Non-figurative compositions and experiments.

**Architecture** — buildings, structures, interiors. From classical architecture to futuristic concepts.

**Animals** — images of animals, birds, fantastic creatures.

**Still Life** — objects, food, flowers, compositions of objects.

## Sorting Within Categories

Each category has sorting options available:

**By date (Fresh)** — newest works first. Allows following content updates.

**By attractiveness (Interesting)** — sorting by prompt popularity. Shows works that are cited more often.

**By views (Popular)** — sorting by number of views. Shows the most viewed works.

**By popularity (Best)** — sorting by number of likes. Shows the best works according to the community.
