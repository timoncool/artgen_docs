# Welcome Bonus

![Welcome Bonus](/assets/docs-pages/screens/daily-reward-popup.png)

Every new ArtGeneration.me user receives a generous welcome bonus that allows you to immediately try all platform features without restrictions.

## What's Included in the Bonus

### 3 Days of PRO Subscription

Full access to all premium platform features for 3 days:

**Increased Limits:**
- 300 generations per day instead of the standard 50
- Ability to generate images in high resolution
- Priority queue — your generations are processed faster

**Extended Access:**
- All generation models, including premium
- All styles without restrictions
- Full set of LoRA models
- Advanced generation parameters

**Additional Features:**
- Face Swap without limitations
- Upscale to maximum resolution
- Priority access to new features

### 100 Non-Expiring Generations

In addition to the subscription, you receive 100 generations that:

- **Don't expire** — remain in your balance until used
- **Don't depend on subscription** — work even after the PRO period ends
- **Accumulate** — can be used at any time

## Bonus Terms

<Callout type="info">
  **No Obligations:** the subscription does not auto-renew, no card required, no hidden fees.
</Callout>

**Activation:**
- The bonus is activated automatically upon registration
- The 3-day countdown starts from the moment of account creation
- Non-expiring generations are credited instantly

## After the Bonus Ends

When the 3 days of PRO subscription end:

**What Remains:**
- Unused non-expiring generations
- All generation history
- Saved images in favorites and folders
- Access to basic platform features

**Basic Access Includes:**
- 50 free generations per day
- Basic generation models
- Basic styles
- Standard resolution

## How to Extend PRO

If you enjoyed the PRO subscription features, you can:

1. Subscribe to a monthly or annual plan
2. Purchase a generation package
3. Participate in promotions and receive bonuses

Read more about pricing in the "Subscriptions" section.

## Tips for Using the Bonus

<Callout>
  - Try different generation models while you have access to all of them
  - Experiment with styles and LoRA
  - Save results you like to favorites
  - Use Face Swap and Upscale for your best works
  - Don't rush — the 100 non-expiring generations will stay with you
</Callout>
